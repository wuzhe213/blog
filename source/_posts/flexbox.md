#### flexbox properties
* flex  
  0-1
* flexDirection 
  * column
  * row
* justifyContent
  * flex-start
  * flex-end
  * center
  * space-between
  * space-around
  * space-evenly
* alignItems
  * alignItems
  * flex-end
  * center
  * stretch
  * baseline


#### Direction
zero point: top-left

1. main direcion  
   set by flexDirection
   justifyContent render along
2. cross direction  
   perpendicular to main direction
   alignItems render along
